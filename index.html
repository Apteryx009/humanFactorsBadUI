Our History

The Retro Cafe has been serving up delicious food and good times in San Marcos, Texas for over 30 years. Founded in 1984 by Keanu Reeves, our shop quickly became a local favorite thanks to our unique retro vibe and mouthwatering menu.

Over the years, we've expanded our menu to include a wide range of dishes that cater to all tastes and dietary needs. From classic burgers and fries to vegan options and gluten-free desserts, we've got something for everyone.

But despite our growth and success, we've never lost sight of our roots. We're still a family-owned and operated business, and we're proud to be a part of the San Marcos community. We believe that good food and good company can bring people together, and we strive to create a welcoming atmosphere where everyone feels at home.

So whether you're a long-time regular or a first-time visitor, we invite you to come and experience The Retro Cafe for yourself. We can't wait to see you!
